About Me

Profile

An ambitious, adaptive and versatile project manager, who is not afraid to tackle challenges in different fields, even if it means starting from zero in order to pursue achievements. Adept at conceptualizing, designing, developing and implementing solutions to compliment business operation and strategy. Capable of discerning and explaining pertinent information to a variety of audiences. Interested in acquiring and increasing technical skills and knowledge in UX/UI designs, full-stack development, analytics, and emerging technology.

Outlook on Life & Work

I want to keep my mind young and sharp as well as my opinions relevant to the evolving society. Similar to work. They say that statistically, people change their career a minimum of 5 times during their professional life. I'm on my fifth, testing the limits of my potential by learning how to code. (Or is this a motive to resist aging?) Could this be it?

Professional Experience

  • Jan 00 - Sept 01

    Project Designer

    My first job!
    At Heath Design Group, Inc., I provided custom and overall interior design solutions, prepared AutoCAD drawings and manual draftings (yes, we did that back in the day), on projects in the Baltimore, MD area.

  • Oct 01 - Jan 04

    Design & Construction Coordinator

    Let the fun begin!
    At CitiFinanical, I was given the opportunity to design (full scope) and remotely manage the construction of over 100 CitiFinancial Branch network throughout the West Coast (incl. Hawaii), Texas, Some Mid-West and Mid-Atlantic states successfully. Apart from multiple small branches all at once, I was assigned larger projects that continue to challenge my professional capabilities.

  • Feb 04 - Aug 12

    Project Manager and Space Analysis & Benchmarking Specialist

    Challenge Accepted!
    My project management and business skills flourished at Citi (Citigroup, N.A.). At first, I contemplated on resigning to pursue a Master's degree. This contemplation was counteredwith an enticing challenges of working on complex and aggressive design & construction projects. They were tough, but I had fun!

  • Nov 12 - Nov 13

    Teacher & Tutor

    I fell in L-O-V-E.
    I didn't fall in love with work, but with the love of my life. It was hard to leave my successful career at Citi, but I had to explore my personal life's potentialand maintain balance. I decided to move, satisfy my heart, see if I can get that Master's degree under my belt, AND experience working abroad. I was looking for a temp job to help a friend at his language school.

  • Nov 13 - Jun 14

    HR Manager: Mobility, Recruitment, Talent Management

    I am too American?!
    Working GDF SUEZ Energy Asia Co., Ltd.in Bangkok, Thailand was a French company with stakes in the utility/ energy companies in Thailand. It was difficult working for a company with a preconceived perception of Americans. But you know what? I wouldn't change a thing about my work ethics. Working hard and smart is key.

  • Nov 14 - Aug 17

    HRD (HR Development) Manager

    Bitten by the Tech bug.
    Aware Technology Solutions Co., Ltd. (ATS) was looking for an HRD Manager to help strategize and develop their biggest asset - the employees. Without having prior experiences in software development, I dove right in, researched, worked with managers, and developed the required criterias and skill sets (technical and soft) to better identify professional improvement opportunities through measureable evaluation methods. My experiences working with so talented people at ATS urged me to explore and venture into the software development field.
